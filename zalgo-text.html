Zalgo text, also known as corrupted or creepy text, is a form of text formatting that uses Unicode combining characters to create an unsettling, chaotic appearance. It overlays special characters above, below, and through normal text to create a "glitched" or "corrupted" visual effect.

Zalgo text is perfect for creating attention-grabbing content, horror-themed designs, gaming usernames, social media posts, memes, and adding a mysterious or supernatural atmosphere to your text. It's popular for Halloween content, horror games, and creative writing projects.

Use Zalgo text when you want to create spooky content, enhance horror-themed projects, make eye-catching social media posts, create unique gaming names, or add a supernatural element to your text. It's especially popular during Halloween or for gothic/horror aesthetics.
